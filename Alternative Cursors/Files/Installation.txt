## Installation ##

1. Place 'cursormenu.swf' and 'cursormenu_settings.txt' in [Skyrim installation folder]/Data/Interface
2. Change the settings in 'cursormenu_settings.txt' to your liking.

## Uninstallation ##

Remove 'cursormenu.swf' and 'cursormenu_settings.txt' from [Skyrim installation folder]/Data/Interface
